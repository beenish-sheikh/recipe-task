"LASSI"

Recipe by Chef tahira

A traditional Indian drink which is also very common in Pakistan is made with yogurt and milk, enjoyed chilled in hot weather and is mostly taken in breakfast.

ingridients:

  • Yogurt ½ kg
  • Milk ½ kg
  • Sugar ½ cup
  • Ice as required

making direction:

  1. In a blender jug add yogurt, milk, sugar and ice and blend well.
  2. Now take out the lassi in glass and serve.
